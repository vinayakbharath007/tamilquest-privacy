# Privacy Policy — Tamil Quest Puzzle

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Tamil Quest Puzzle is a free mobile game developed by TamilQuest.
This app is designed to work offline and does not require an account.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Tamil Quest Puzzle does not collect, store, or share any personal
information. We do not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Payment information

## Data Storage

All game progress (levels completed, coins, settings) is stored
locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device
and is not transmitted to any server.

## Third Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools,
advertising networks, or social media platforms.

## Children's Privacy

Tamil Quest Puzzle is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly
collect any information from children or adults.
